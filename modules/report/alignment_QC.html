Gene Body Coverage
The plot shows the relative abundance of sequencing read across the gene length from 5' to 3'. It is a good indicator that whether reads have bias toward one or both ends, which could be caused by RNA degradation.
Junction Saturation
The curves show the diversity of annotated and noval splic junctions. The former reveals if there are diverse RNA population in the sequencing sample, thus if the sample has enough depth. The later indicates how many new, un-annotated junctions are revealed by increasing number of sequencing reads.
Splicing Junction
The pie chart shows the percentage of annotated and novel junctions presented in the sample.
ReadPair Inner Distance
The histogram shows the distribution of inner distance (between two closest ends) of the reads pairs estimated from the alignment. The accurate inner distance cannot be estimated from the single end sequencing data
Read Dupliction
The plot shows the read duplication level and theoretical duplication level
